ABOUT US

Kridathon aims to provide a portal where players of a game may make a team online and decide a place where they can organize their matches. This will help all their players who are unable to find teammates for their game.Whenever there is a tournament, it is very difficult to organize a game as it needs proper planning of matches and rounds. This project aims to make fixtures for the games and all rules that must be followed.This project aims to provide a platform for inter-school, inter-district or inter-college tournaments for exposure of games and health in our society, basically among the children.Many of the players remain unaware of the events going around them. This project aims to provide them the complete information about all types of competitions going around them in which they can participate.
